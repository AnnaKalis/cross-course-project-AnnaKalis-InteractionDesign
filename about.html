About us

Rainy days is a leading provider and developer of outdoor and technical jackets.

We offer a wide range of sustainable outdoor jackets that are designed to help you enjoy the great outdoors in a responsible and eco-friendly way.

Our commitment to sustainability is at the core of everything we do. We believe that outdoor gears hould not come at the expense of the planet, which is why we use only high-quality, sustainable materials in all of our products.

From organic cotton to recycled polyester, we carefully source ourmaterials to ensure that they are eco-friendly and have a low impact on the environment.

“We keep materials as pure as possible. We never mix natural and synthetic fibers.”

“We focus on longevity of our product. It is the most important sustainability initiative we can take.”

Our team of designers and outdoor enthusiasts work tirelessly to create jackets that are not only stylish and functional, but also socially responsible.

We take pride in partnering with ethical suppliers who prioritize fair labor practices and ethical sourcing. We believe that it's important to support businesses that share our values, and we're committed to being a part of the solution to creating amore sustainable future.

At our shop, you'll find a variety of sustainable outdoor jackets that are perfect for any outdoor activity, from hiking and camping to skiing and snowboarding. Our jackets are designed to keep you warm, dry, and comfortable in any weather, without compromising on style or performance.
